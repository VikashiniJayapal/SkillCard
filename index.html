Here are the types of Cards available for application upon successful completion of the CITB Health, Safety & Environment Test , often referred to as the Touch Screen Test.

CSCS Cards

GREEN LABOURER CARD

The Labourer card is designed specifically for individuals engaged in laboring duties. This card is initially valid for five years and cannot be renewed.

BLACK MANAGERS CARD

The CSCS Black Card is specifically designed for professionals in managerial and technical roles within the construction industry.

BLUE SKILLED WORKER CARD

To obtain this card, all applicants must have passed the CITB Health, Safety, and Environment test at the relevant level for the specific occupation being applied.

EXPERIENCED WORKER CARD

The CSCS Red Provisional Card is a temporary certification that has a one-year validity and cannot be renewed. It is designed for skilled workers with on job experience.

GOLD SKILLED WORKER

The CSCS Gold Card is a valuable certification for skilled construction professionals, with a standard validity of five years, and the option for renewal before its expiry date.

PROVISIONAL RED CARD

The CSCS Provisional Card is tailored for individuals who are in the midst of probationary periods, during which employers evaluate their suitability for employment.

CPCS Cards

CPCS RED CARD CARD

To obtain a Red Trained Operator Card, individuals need to pass a Health, Safety, and Environment test, followed by the CPCS Theory Test within two years.

CPCS BLUE CARD

The CPCS Blue Card is designed for experienced and skilled plant operators in the construction industry, indicating their high proficiency.

CSIRS Cards

CSIRS LABOURER CARD

Scaffolding Laborers play a crucial role in the construction industry, supporting the safe and efficient assembly, alteration & dismantling of scaffolding structures.

CISRS TRAINEE SCAFFOLDER

The CISRS Trainee Scaffolder Card is a certification available to individuals who are in the process of completing the necessary training to obtain this card.

SCAFFOLDER (TUBE & FITTING)

A CISRS Scaffolder Card is a certification that signifies an individual's qualification to lead a scaffold gang and conduct basic scaffold operations.
